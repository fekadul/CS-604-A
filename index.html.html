About Us

Doctor Colosseum is a web development based software. It's mission is to help all people effectively select a healthcare professional that would help them best with their health related issue(s). This is done by gathering scores on procedures done by doctors, calculation of procedures, comparison and displaying up-to-date scores on procedures completed by them as well as comparing doctors to one another to determine which are the champions of a procedure or practice at any given time.
